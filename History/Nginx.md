Igor Sysoev is the creator and original author of the Nginx web server, which he developed to solve the C10K problem (handling 10,000+ concurrent connections). He began development in 2002 and publicly released it in October 2004. Nginx is now a widely used, high-performance web server, reverse proxy, and load balancer. 
Key Facts About the Author:

- Background: Sysoev is a Russian software engineer who developed Nginx while working at the Russian internet company Rambler.
- Development: He started the project to improve upon existing Apache server limitations.
- Company Formation: In 2011, he founded Nginx, Inc. to support the software, which was later acquired by F5, Inc. in 2019 for $670 million.
- Departure: Sysoev left F5 and the Nginx project in January 2022 to pursue other interests.
- Legacy: Nginx is currently one of the most popular web servers globally, used for its speed, security, and scalability.
